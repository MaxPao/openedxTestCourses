Welcome!

We're glad to welcome you to DAT206x: Analyzing and Visualizing Data with Excel!

Everyday, millions of people use Excel for their daily tasks. Whether is for number crunching, reporting, calculations, you name it. In this course you will learn how to use Excel to perform data analysis and visualizations. Specifically, you will learn how to bring data from other sources into Excel, perform data cleaning and pre-processing steps, create data mash-ups, build your business logic in the Excel data model, and present the data with interactive visualizations.

We welcome all learners! While you might find understanding of Excel analytic tools such as Excel tables, pivot tables and pivot charts, and experience in working with data from databases helpful, we're sure that everyone can follow this course. If you're a novice user, or just stuck with something, check out the discussion forum. If you're a more advanced learner, or already a seasoned Excel user, stick around the discussion group and perhaps help your fellow learners. 

We hope you'll have an enriching and engaging learning experience!
