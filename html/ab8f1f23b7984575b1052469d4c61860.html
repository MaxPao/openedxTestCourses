Applying Conditional Formatting

Credit to WLitzenberg

"Conditional Formatting is not "tested" - its just there to show you another way to visualize your data - in this case, to highlight which State or States (really provinces) VanArsdel share is less than the overall VanArsdel's share for the country."

In general:

    • select the cells you want to conditionally format
    • from the Home tab click on the Conditional Formatting Icon
    • from the Conditional Formatting menu, select Highlight Cells Rules and then Less Than...

    • after you select the Less Than... option, you should get dialog box similar to that shown below - replace the value shown with the cell reference for the Grand Total of the isVanArsdel Y column (in my case this was C10), change the formatting if you like and click on OK
